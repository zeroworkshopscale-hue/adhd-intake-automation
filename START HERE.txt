==========================================================
  ADHD Intake Automation  —  How to start
==========================================================

TO OPEN THE APP
---------------
Double-click either of these:
  * "ADHD Intake Automation" shortcut on your Desktop, OR
  * "Launch ADHD Intake.bat" inside this folder.

The app will:
  1. Ask for your OSCAR username and password (so uploads go under
     YOUR provider profile). Type them in and click "Sign in".
  2. Open the dashboard. Drag questionnaire PDFs from Outlook (or your
     files) onto the big drop area. You can also click it to browse.

Processed patients show up in the table at the bottom with their
Demographic Number, Name, and Email.


YOUR PATIENT SHEET
------------------
By default the app keeps its OWN spreadsheet for you at:
    data\intake_master_copy.csv
Click the "Open copy sheet" button in the app to open it, then copy the
rows in one go into your master Google Sheet (same column order).
(If you'd rather the app write straight into a Google Sheet instead, see
the README - set sheets.mode to "google".)


BEFORE THE FIRST REAL UPLOAD  (one-time setup)
----------------------------------------------
The OSCAR address is already set to your KAI EMR
(https://welcome.kai-oscar.com/kaiemr). You do NOT put your OSCAR
password in any file - the app asks for it each time it starts.

For scanned / handwritten forms, also install "Tesseract-OCR for
Windows" (free). Typed/fillable PDFs work without it.

Tune the signature check to YOUR forms once: take one signed and one
blank consent PDF and run, in this folder:
    python tools\calibrate_signature.py --signed signed.pdf --unsigned blank.pdf
then put the number it recommends into config.yaml (validation:
min_ink_density). This stops real signatures being rejected by mistake.


WHAT HAPPENS TO EACH FILE
-------------------------
  * If the consent page is NOT signed  -> the file is rejected and moved
    to the "data\rejected" folder. Nothing is uploaded. The app then pops
    up a ready-to-send reminder email - click "Copy email text", paste it
    into Outlook, and send it to the patient.
  * If the patient is NOT found in OSCAR -> it stops and is marked
    "Patient Not Found". Nothing is uploaded.
  * Otherwise -> it is uploaded to OSCAR Documents (Type = Form,
    Description = "ADHD Assessment Tool"), added to your copy sheet, and
    moved to "data\processed".

Everything is recorded locally in a database with a full audit trail.
Logs are in the "logs" folder if anything goes wrong.
